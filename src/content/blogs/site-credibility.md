## The Number That Changes Everything

In 2006, researchers at Carleton University found that visual appeal can be assessed within 50 milliseconds.

That first impression is formed before a visitor reads your case studies, checks your pricing, or evaluates your credentials.

## Design Is Credibility. Full Stop.

Visitors do not evaluate your actual credibility first. They scan visual elements and extrapolate everything else.

A polished, cohesive design leads visitors to assume the entire experience behind it is equally credible.

## The 5 Credibility Signals AI Reads First

- Visual design quality.
- Load speed.
- Structured, scannable content.
- Author identity and expertise.
- Third-party mentions.

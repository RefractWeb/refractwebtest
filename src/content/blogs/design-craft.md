## The Problem with Fine

Most websites on the internet are fine. They load, have a nav, a hero section, some copy, and a contact form.

Fine does not fail spectacularly. It quietly underperforms, month after month, while the business owner assumes the problem is traffic.

## What We Build by Default

- Motion and 3D that communicate the site is alive.
- Components and cards designed to a proven standard of what performs.
- ICP research that shapes hierarchy, copy, typography, and layout density.

> Good enough is a choice. We just choose not to make it.
